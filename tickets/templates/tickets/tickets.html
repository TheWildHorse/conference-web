Acquiring tickets

Tickets are sold via Entrio online shop. You can pay by credit card (Mastercard, Maestro, Visa, Amex, Diners) or bank transfer. The latter may take a few days to process though.

There's only one category of tickets, which will get you entrance to both conference days and includes a T-shirt and a swag bag with goodies. The price of the ticket is 500HRK (~€65).

We will host a number of quality talks including two keyspeakers. Furthermore, this year we introduced a concept of popular vote, which means that ticket holders voted on three more talks to be included in an official talks roster.

If you don't want to pay for a ticket, submit a talk to one of our user groups today: Frontman Zagreb, Javascript Zagreb, Lambda Zagreb, Microsoft Community Hrvatska, Python Hrvatska, RubyZG, Web Designers Hrvatska or ZgPHP. All speakers who speak at a meetup before the conference will get a free ticket.

By buying a ticket, you agree to follow our Code of Conduct.

Tickets are in limited number so get them while you can!
