Acquiring tickets

Tickets are sold via Entrio online shop. You can pay by credit card (Mastercard, Maestro, Visa, Amex, Diners) or bank transfer.

There's only one category of tickets, which will get you entrance to both conference days and includes a T-shirt and a swag bag with goodies. The price of the ticket is 300kn (~€40) during the early bird period which ends on June 26th. After that the price of the ticket will be 500kn (~€65).

We will host a number of quality talks. Last year we introduced the concept of the popular vote, which means that ticket holders will get a chance to vote on a number of talks to be included in the official talks roster.

If you don't want to pay for a ticket, submit a talk to one of our user groups today: {% for ug in usergroups %} {% if not forloop.first and not forloop.last %},{% endif %} {% if forloop.last %}, or {% endif %} {{ ug.name }} {% endfor %}. All speakers who speak at one of our meetups in the period since WebCamp Zagreb 2015 until this year's conference will get a free ticket.

By buying a ticket, you agree to follow our Code of Conduct.

Tickets are in limited number so get them while you can!
